Rolling and Sipping

Two things I really really enjoy!

This is a collage of fond and funny memories on the theme of rolling and sipping.

Over the years I have ridden several bikes and in many places - Washington State and North Carolina USA, Ireland, Gemany, Italy, and Mallorca Spain.

And in my sipping conquests near to and away from the bike, I have had some very interesting spellings of my name. Who would have thought that Julie is so hard.
